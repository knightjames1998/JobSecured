SMS Terms

Effective date: July 8, 2026

These terms cover text messages sent by JobSecured on behalf of the local service businesses whose phone lines we answer, and messages sent to our own business clients.

Program description

JobSecured sends transactional and service-related text messages, including:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders for callers who book service with a participating business
  • Call summary notifications delivered to the business client whose line was answered
  • Follow-up service messages, such as review requests, where the business's plan includes them

We do not send promotional marketing blasts, and consent to receive service messages is never a condition of purchasing anything.

How consent works

When a caller books an appointment or requests a callback, our assistant asks whether they would like to receive a text message at the number they provided. Messages are sent only if the caller agrees. Business clients provide written consent to receive call summary texts as part of their service agreement. Consent can be withdrawn at any time by replying STOP.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on call and appointment activity. Typically 1 to 3 messages per booked appointment for callers, and one summary message per answered call for business clients.

Fees

Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile carrier plan. JobSecured does not charge callers for text messages.

Reply STOP at any time to cancel and stop receiving messages. Reply HELP for assistance, or contact us at james@jobsecured.co or (210) 500-0096.

Opt out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out. You will receive one final message confirming your opt-out, and no further messages will be sent to your number. You can re-subscribe by providing your number again when booking service.

Support

Privacy

Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties. Mobile information is never sold or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details on how information is collected and used.
